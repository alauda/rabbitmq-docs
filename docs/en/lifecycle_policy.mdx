# Lifecycle Policy

## Version Lifecycle Timeline

Below is the lifecycle schedule for released versions of the Alauda Streaming Service for RabbitMQ:

| Version | Release Date | End of Support                                    |
|:--------|:-------------|:--------------------------------------------------|
| v4.0.x  | 2025-04-08   | Supported until the End of Life (EOL) of ACP 4.0. |

## Release Policy

* A new patch version of Alauda Streaming Service for RabbitMQ is released as needed to address bugs and security vulnerabilities.

## Maintenance Policy

Alauda provides maintenance support for the Alauda Streaming Service for RabbitMQ based on its rolling support model.

During the support period of each version, Alauda provides the following services:

- **Security Updates**: Regular patches and updates in line with Alauda security standards.
- **Bug Fixes**: Timely fixes for critical and major issues, aligned with upstream RabbitMQ community fixes.
- **Upgrade Assistance**: Guidance and support for smooth upgrades between Alauda Streaming Service for RabbitMQ versions.
- **Regular Maintenance**: Periodic updates to ensure the operator remains stable and compatible with the Kubernetes ecosystem.
